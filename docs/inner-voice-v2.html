Make ideas feel worth returning to.

V1 proved the system: private notes, search, AI recall, persistence, logs, backups, and tests. V2 changes how the same ideas are felt and explored. This is a side workflow; the original build stages remain in inner-voice.html.

Connect with your ideas instead of writing them down and leaving them behind.

Current Product Direction

This is not a general notebook, task manager, shopping list, bookmark archive, or read-later inbox. It is a private place for your own ideas and meaningful ideas discovered elsewhere.

Ideas feel valuable

Each idea should stand on the page like gold without using literal gold, glass cases, frames, or museum decoration.

Soft summer atmosphere

Air, blue daylight, gentle motion, quiet depth, and spacious composition should make browsing feel light and alive.

Low cognitive load

Show fewer ideas, reduce permanent controls, and avoid dashboard density, instructional copy, and feature explanations.

Reconnection before organization

Resurface forgotten ideas and support rough-memory search. The user should not spend time building folders or manually categorizing.

Alive but unhurried

Hover and cycling may shift light, clarity, or depth. Avoid bounce, dramatic scaling, glowing effects, and attention-seeking motion.

Private by default

The collection remains personal. Later sharing must expose only the explicitly chosen idea or result.

Signature Interaction: The Living Field

Ideas are living intellectual objects that awaken when approached. This interaction system, not decoration, should make the collection feel valuable, responsive, and unique to this product.

Rest

Ideas wait quietly across a spacious, continuous field. They are readable and distinct without becoming uniform cards or rows.

Approach

As the pointer approaches, an idea gains clarity, reveals more of itself, and receives slightly more space. Nearby ideas yield without becoming hidden.

Hover

The idea fully awakens: text reaches full contrast, quiet context appears, and one clear open affordance becomes visible. Avoid bounce, glow, dramatic scale, and ornamental movement.

Open

The selected idea expands in its existing place while the rest of the field recedes. Full content and secondary actions appear without navigating to a generic detail page.

Search And Recall

Relevant ideas gather into focus while unrelated ideas recede. Clearing the query restores the previous field and scroll position.

Scroll

Ideas enter through controlled spatial rhythm. Partial ideas near viewport edges create curiosity, while motion remains calm, interruptible, and accessible.

An idea should feel collectible, touchable, and alive before the user studies every control.

Scope Guardrails

  • “Museum” is a metaphor for value, not a literal visual theme.
  • The temporary product name is Idea Store.
  • Do not build the Chrome extension during this design workflow.
  • Do not build Idea Rooms, voice, game levels, or sharing yet.
  • Do not redesign backend architecture while testing visual ideas.
  • Do not accept one mockup as the answer; iterate and compare.
  • Do not confuse premium design with serif type, empty space, or decorative atmosphere alone.

Future Vision Archive

These ideas are intentionally preserved but deferred. They must not expand the current redesign slice.

One-click idea capture

Highlight text on the web, right-click, and save it with source metadata. Never save an entire page as a bookmark.

Automatic understanding

AI creates titles, tags, categories, and relationships without asking the user to organize the collection manually.

Immersive Idea Rooms

A dark, full-screen journey lets one idea develop through continuous cinematic scenes and saved progress.

Creative guide

An initially quiet AI presence asks one short question at a time. Optional avatars and voice may later reduce loneliness.

Living decision patterns

User choices quietly form a hidden visual structure. The journey stays non-technical and avoids forms, specifications, and jargon.

Possible futures

The room may explore current trends, idea synergy, needs, simulations, objections, and alternative directions.

Final reveal

Completing a journey produces an emotional visual result that helps the user imagine the product, not a technical build spec.

Intentional sharing

Everything remains private unless the user explicitly shares one chosen idea or completed result.
